Welcome to Girum Wanoro’s Page

About Me

My name is Girum Wanoro. I was born and raised in Ethiopia and moved to the United States when I was 12 years old. I’m currently a student at the University of Washington, majoring in Geography with Data Science and minoring in Business Administration. Growing up in an immigrant family taught me resilience, independence, and the importance of community.

  • First-generation college student
  • Fluent in three languages
  • Passionate about data, culture, and community empowerment

My Interests & Goals

I’ve always been curious about how people and places connect. My academic work in geography has helped me understand how data can tell stories about human experience — from mapping migration to studying social patterns. In the future, I hope to use data science to create positive change in underserved communities and promote equity through education and access.
