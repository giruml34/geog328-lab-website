About Girum

My name is — Girum Wanoro. I’m a student at the University of Washington studying Geography with Data Science and minoring in Business Administration.

I was born and raised in Ethiopia and moved to the United States at age 12. That journey shaped the way I learn and create — blending technical skills with cultural understanding. I’m interested in using data and design to tell stories about people, place, and community.
