About Girum

This page is about me — Girum Wanoro. I’m a student at the University of Washington studying Geography with Data Science and minoring in Business Administration. I created this website as part of GEOG 328 to showcase what I learned about building a responsive webpage using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I was born and raised in Ethiopia and moved to the United States at 12 years old. My journey has shaped the way I approach learning and creativity — combining technical skills with cultural understanding. Through this class, I’ve learned how design and data visualization can help communicate ideas in clear and engaging ways.

This website reflects who I am — curious, creative, and always learning something new.
